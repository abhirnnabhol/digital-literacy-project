Part 2: 8-Point "Stay Safe Online" Checklist
Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Use apps like Google Authenticator for all professional accounts (LinkedIn, GitHub, Gmail).

Verify the Source: Never click links or download attachments from unknown or suspicious email addresses.

Check for HTTPS: Only enter personal data on websites that have a "padlock" icon and start with https://.

Use Unique Passwords: Use a different, complex password for every platform. Avoid using common words or your name.

Secure Your Network: Avoid using public or "Open" Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks like banking or accessing university portals.

Keep Software Updated: Regularly update your OS, browser, and tools like VS Code to ensure you have the latest security patches.

Monitor Account Activity: Periodically check the "Device Activity" section in your Google and LinkedIn settings to spot any unrecognized logins.

Report Fraud Immediately: If you suspect a scam, report it to the National Cybercrime Reporting Portal (Helpline: 1930) or the university IT cell.
